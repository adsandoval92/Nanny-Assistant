Nanny Assistant

An all-encompassing business organization and management solution for caregivers. The Nanny Assistant will help you organize your business, your time, and your money.

Main Features:

Calendar and Scheduling Assistant

  • Keep your clients in sync with you schedule with a shared calendar.
  • Manage event requests and add new events via the Scheduling Assistant.

Live News Feed

  • Keep in touch with your clients with a live news feed that can be easily viewed while they are away.
  • Comment on activities posted to the news feed to share your thoughts.

Profiles

  • Easy access to important information about your clients and their children.
  • Emergency contact information and procedures when you need it.

Time Sheet

  • Keep track of your hours and income with each client.
